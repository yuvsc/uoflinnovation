About E & I

The University of Louisville E&I (Entrepreneurship and Innovation Club) aims to build a diverse environment of innovation and foster entrepreneurship at the university. We empower students to build on their unique ideas and passions, leveraging cross departmental collaboration and teamwork to cultivate a diverse community of innovative thinkers (or innovators).

University Innovation Fellows Program

This program is hosted by Stanford and Epicenter. It allows students to learn from professional innovators throughout a period of six weeks to help bring innovative ideas to the university.

  • Spring 2016

    Beginning of UIF at UofL

    Four students from the University of Louisville were inducted as fellows last spring and have already begun to impact the entrepreneurial mindset at the university. So far they have already started an Entrepreneurship and Innovation Club, held design thinking workshops aimed at product innovation, and auditioned for America’s Greatest Makers, a TV show by Intel focusing on building innovative solution to everyday problems.

  • Spring 2017

    First E&I UIF Fellows

    These four students from the University of Louisville were inducted as University Innovation Fellow. With the help of the past fellows, they have been able to become change makers on campus. They have been involved with the Entrepreneurship and Innovation Club. They plan on increasing awareness of opportunities on campus for aspiring entrepreneurs and innovators by showing students resources during orientation. Their hope is to encourage innovation on campus.
